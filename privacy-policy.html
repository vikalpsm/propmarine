Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 07, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At PropMarine Realty, we collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out enquiry forms, request proposals, or communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Details about your real estate requirements, budget, preferences, and interests.
  • Information provided during calls, WhatsApp chats, or emails with our relationship managers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, personalize, and improve our services as a real estate sales channel partner.
  • To share project brochures, layout maps, pricing sheets, and unit availability with you.
  • To coordinate site visits and match you with property developers and brokers.
  • To send you transactional messages, updates, and project communications about relevant opportunities.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

As a sales channel partner, we act as a bridge between you (the home buyer/investor) and property developers/brokers. We may share your enquiry information with:

  • The developers or builders of the specific projects you have enquired about, so they can verify and process bookings.
  • Our authorized sales executives and channel partner networks to coordinate site visits and facilitate deal closures.
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our CRM, WhatsApp tools, or website analytics.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for independent promotional purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies, tracking pixels (such as Google Analytics and Meta Pixels), and similar technologies to analyze site traffic, understand user behavior, and improve our digital communication. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some website features may function differently as a result.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no transmission over the internet or database storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or ask us to stop sending you promotional updates. You can opt out at any time by contacting us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any updates.
